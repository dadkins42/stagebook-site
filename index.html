Features

Everything you need
before, during, and after the show.

Built for musicians who take their craft seriously — without making things complicated.

📄

Song Library

Store lyrics with key, tempo, vibe, tags, and notes. Search instantly. Filter by tag or favorite. Your whole catalog, organized.

🎵

Smart Formatting

Use section headers like [Chorus] and [Verse 1]. StageBook auto-indents chorus sections and highlights chords inline — no extra work.

🎤

Voice Recordings

Record takes directly in the app, attached to the song. Import audio files too. Never lose a great idea between rehearsals.

🔗

Streaming Links

Attach Spotify, Apple Music, YouTube, or Bandcamp links to any song. Auto-detects the service. Opens in the right app with one tap.

🎸

Chord Transpose

Shift chords up or down by semitone with a tap. Change the key on the fly without editing the song. Perfect for different vocalists or tunings.

🌑

Full Screen Mode

Tap expand for a clean, distraction-free view. Big text, no UI clutter. Readable under stage lights at 2am.

Band Features

Built for the whole band, not just you.

Create a band, invite your members, and stay coordinated — setlists, rehearsals, and songs, all in sync.

📋

Setlists & Shows

Build and share setlists with the whole band. Everyone has the same running order — keys, tempos, and notes included.

🥁

Rehearsal Planning

Schedule rehearsals with a song agenda. Members know what you're working on before they walk in the door.

🎧

Playlists

Group songs by mood or gig type — Openers, Slow Burn, Crowd Requests. Ready when you need them.

📌

Quick References

Stash stage plots, gear settings, and sound check cues. Everything the band needs on the night, in one place.
