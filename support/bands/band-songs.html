🎵 Band Songs

Your band's shared song library — one place for every song your band plays, with lyrics and chords for everyone.

Your Band's Song Library

The Band Songs library is separate from your personal song collection. It contains every song your band performs or is learning, and it's shared with all band members automatically.

Browse, search, and filter your band's repertoire to quickly find what you need. Each song shows its lyrics, chords, key, tempo, and any notes your band has added.

📱
Screenshot: Band song library

Adding Songs to the Band

Add songs to the band library by creating them from scratch or by copying songs from your personal library. When you copy a personal song, the band gets its own version that members can edit independently.

You can also import songs from Song Lab once they're ready for the full band. This keeps your workflow clean — experiment in the lab, then promote to the band library when it's polished.

📱
Screenshot: Adding a song to the band
💡 Tip

Use tags like "covers" and "originals" to keep your band library organized as it grows. You can filter by tag to quickly find the right songs for any situation.

Sharing with Members

Every song in the band library is automatically available to all band members through iCloud sync. When someone updates lyrics or adds a note, everyone sees the change on their own device.

No more emailing lyric sheets or texting chord charts. Everyone always has the latest version right in StageBook.
