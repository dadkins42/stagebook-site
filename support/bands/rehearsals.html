🗓️ Rehearsals

Plan productive rehearsals with agendas, song lists, and notes so everyone shows up prepared.

Scheduling a Rehearsal

Tap the + button on the Rehearsals tab to schedule a new session. Set the date, time, and location so every band member knows when and where to show up.

Rehearsals appear on your band's shared timeline, making it easy to see what's coming up at a glance.

📱
Screenshot: Scheduling a rehearsal
💡 Tip

Schedule rehearsals well in advance so everyone can plan around them. Consistent rehearsal times help build good habits.

Creating an Agenda

A great rehearsal starts with a plan. Add songs from your band library to the rehearsal agenda so everyone knows what you'll be working on. You can add notes to each song — like "focus on the bridge" or "try a new arrangement."

Prioritize the songs that need the most work, and save the ones you've nailed for a quick run-through at the end.

📱
Screenshot: Rehearsal agenda with song notes

During Rehearsal

When rehearsal starts, StageBook puts your agenda front and center. Work through songs one by one, and jot down notes as you go. Capture ideas, flag trouble spots, and record quick audio snippets right from the rehearsal view.

After rehearsal, your notes are saved and ready for next time. No more forgetting what you worked on or what needs attention.
