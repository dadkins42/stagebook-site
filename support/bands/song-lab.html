🧪 Song Lab

A creative workspace for songs in progress. Experiment with new material before it's ready for the full band.

What is Song Lab?

Song Lab is a dedicated space for songs that aren't quite ready for prime time. Think of it as your band's creative workshop — a place to sketch out ideas, try new arrangements, and develop material without cluttering your main song library.

Songs in the lab are visible to all band members, so everyone can contribute ideas and feedback as new material takes shape.

📱
Screenshot: Song Lab overview
💡 Tip

Use Song Lab for cover songs you're still learning too — not just originals. Move them to the band library once everyone has their parts down.

Working on New Material

Create a new song in the lab and start building it out. Add rough lyrics, chord ideas, structure notes, and even quick audio recordings of riff ideas. Everything stays together in one place.

Band members can each add their own notes and ideas to lab songs. It's a collaborative space where the best version of the song can emerge naturally.

📱
Screenshot: Editing a song in Song Lab

Moving Songs to the Band Library

When a song is ready, promote it from Song Lab to your band's main song library with a single tap. The song keeps all its lyrics, chords, notes, and recordings — nothing gets lost in the move.

Once promoted, the song is available for setlists, rehearsal agendas, and everything else in your band workflow. The lab version is removed to keep things tidy.
