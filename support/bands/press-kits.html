📰 Press Kits

Build a professional electronic press kit right in StageBook — everything venues and promoters ask for, ready to share.

Building Your EPK

An electronic press kit (EPK) is your band's resume. StageBook makes it easy to put one together by guiding you through each section — bio, photos, song list, and media links.

Having a polished EPK ready to go means you can respond to booking inquiries quickly and professionally. No scrambling to put materials together at the last minute.

📱
Screenshot: Press kit builder overview

Band Bio and Photos

Write your band's bio directly in StageBook. Keep it concise and engaging — a short paragraph about who you are, what you play, and what makes your band unique. You can maintain multiple versions for different contexts.

Add your best band photos to the press kit. High-quality images make a strong first impression on venues and promoters.

📱
Screenshot: Editing band bio and photos
💡 Tip

Keep two versions of your bio — a short one (2-3 sentences) for quick pitches and a longer one for formal submissions. Venues appreciate having options.

Song List and Media

Showcase your repertoire by adding a curated song list to your press kit. Pick your strongest songs — you don't need to list everything, just the highlights that show what your band can do.

Add links to your music on streaming platforms, YouTube videos, or SoundCloud tracks. Venues want to hear you before they book you, so make it easy for them.

📱
Screenshot: Song list and media links in press kit

Sharing Your Press Kit

When your EPK is ready, share it with a single tap. StageBook generates a clean, professional link you can send to venues, promoters, and booking agents. They can view everything in their browser — no app required.

Update your press kit anytime and the shared link always shows the latest version. Keep your materials fresh without having to resend anything.
