🎪 Shows

Keep every gig organized from booking to showtime. Track venues, manage setlists, and never forget a detail.

Adding a Show

Creating a new show is quick and easy. Tap the + button on the Shows tab and fill in the basics — venue name, date, time, and any notes you want to remember. You can always come back and update the details as things get confirmed.

Each show gets its own page where you can see everything at a glance, from load-in times to the setlist your band will play.

📱
Screenshot: Adding a new show

Show Details

Every show has a details section where you can store all the information your band needs. Add the venue address, contact info, set times, pay details, and any special notes about the gig.

Having all this info in one place means everyone in the band knows exactly what to expect. No more digging through group chats to find the load-in time.

📱
Screenshot: Show details view
💡 Tip

Add the venue address to your show details so band members can quickly get directions on the day of the gig.

Setlists for Shows

Build a setlist for each show by pulling songs from your band's song library. Drag and drop to reorder songs, and organize them into sets if you're playing multiple rounds.

Your setlist stays attached to the show, so you can look back at past gigs and see exactly what you played. Great for avoiding repeats at the same venue.

📱
Screenshot: Building a setlist

Day of Show

When it's showtime, StageBook puts your setlist front and center. Swipe through songs as you play, and everyone in the band sees the same setlist on their own device.

Focus on playing — StageBook handles the rest. Your lyrics, chords, and notes are all right there when you need them.
