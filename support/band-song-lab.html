The Song Lab

A workspace for songs in progress — from rough ideas to show-ready material.

What Is Song Lab?

Song Lab is a shared workspace for songs that aren't ready for the main band library yet. It's where your band can collect ideas, work on arrangements, and develop new material without cluttering the main song list.

📸Screenshot: The Song Lab workspace

Working on Ideas

Add rough ideas, covers you're considering, or originals in progress. Attach notes, recordings, and reference charts. Everyone in the band can contribute and comment.

Promoting to Band Songs

When a song is ready for the stage, promote it from Song Lab to Band Songs. This moves it into your official shared library where it can be added to setlists and rehearsal agendas.

Tip

Keep your main band library clean by using Song Lab for everything that's still in progress. Only promote songs when they're truly show-ready.
