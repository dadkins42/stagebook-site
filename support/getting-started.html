Getting Started

Everything you need to know to start using StageBook in the first five minutes.

Download & Install

StageBook is available for iPhone, iPad, and Mac. Download it free from the App Store.

📱Screenshot: App Store download page

Your Song Library

When you first open StageBook, you'll see your song library. This is home base — where all your songs live. Tap the + button to add your first song.

📸Screenshot: Empty song library with + button highlighted

Adding Your First Song

Enter a title, paste or type your lyrics, and optionally add details like key, tempo, and tags. Hit Save and your song is stored locally on your device.

📸Screenshot: Song editor with sample content
Tip

StageBook works fully offline. Your songs are stored on your device and never require an internet connection to access.

What's Next?

  • Songs — Learn about formatting, tags, search, and full screen mode
  • Charts — Attach chord charts and lead sheets to your songs
  • Recordings — Record ideas and attach audio to songs
  • Bands — Create a band and start collaborating with your members
