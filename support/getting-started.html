🚀 Getting Started

New to StageBook? This guide will walk you through the basics so you can start using the app right away.

Welcome to StageBook

StageBook is your personal music companion — a place to store lyrics, charts, recordings, and everything you need on stage or in the rehearsal room. It works entirely offline, so your content is always available, even without an internet connection.

🎬
Video: Quick tour of StageBook

Your Song Library

The first thing you'll see is your song library. This is where all your songs live — lyrics, chords, charts, and recordings all attached to each song.

Adding Your First Song

Tap the + button to create a new song. Give it a title, add your lyrics, and you're done. You can always come back to add more details later.

📱
Screenshot: Adding a new song

Organizing with Tags

Use tags to organize your songs however makes sense to you — by genre, by set, by key, or anything else. Tags make it easy to filter and find songs quickly.

💡 Tip

You don't have to organize everything right away. Start by adding songs, and add tags later as you figure out what groupings work best for you.

iCloud Sync

StageBook can sync your songs across all your Apple devices using iCloud. Turn on iCloud sync in Settings to keep your iPhone, iPad, and Mac all in sync automatically.

Your songs are stored as simple Markdown files, so they're always readable and portable — you own your data.
