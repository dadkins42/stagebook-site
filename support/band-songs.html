Your Band Songs

Your band's shared song library — the single source of truth for what you play.

The Shared Library

Band Songs is your band's shared song library. Every member sees the same list of songs, with the same details, charts, and notes. When someone updates a song, the change is visible to everyone.

📸Screenshot: The band songs library

Adding Songs to the Band

Any band member can add songs to the shared library. You can create a new song from scratch or copy one from your personal library into the band.

Personal vs. Band Songs

Your personal song library and the band song library are separate. Personal songs are yours alone — only you can see them. Band songs are shared with every member. You might have the same song in both places with different notes or charts.

Charts in Band Songs

Band songs can have shared charts that every member sees. For details on how personal and band charts work together, see Understanding Charts.
