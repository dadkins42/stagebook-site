Your Songs

Your personal song library — the heart of StageBook.

Adding Songs

Tap the + button to create a new song. Enter a title and your lyrics. You can also add metadata like key, tempo, artist, album, and tags.

📸Screenshot: Adding a new song

Formatting Your Lyrics

Use section headers to organize your lyrics. Wrap section names in square brackets and StageBook will automatically format them.

Section Headers

Type headers like [Verse 1], [Chorus], [Bridge], etc. Chorus sections are automatically indented so they stand out visually.

📸Screenshot: Formatted lyrics with section headers

Inline Chords

Add chords directly in your lyrics. StageBook highlights them so they're easy to spot while performing.

Tags & Organization

Add tags to songs to organize them by genre, mood, era, or anything else. Use tags to quickly filter your library.

📸Screenshot: Tag filter chips

Search & Sort

Search your entire library instantly by title, artist, or lyrics content. Sort by title, date added, or date modified.

Favorites

Tap the heart icon to mark songs as favorites. Filter your library to show only favorites when you need quick access to your go-to songs.

Full Screen Mode

Tap the expand icon for a clean, distraction-free view of your lyrics. Big text, no UI clutter — perfect for reading on stage under dim lights.

📸Screenshot: Full screen lyrics view

Transpose

Shift all chords in a song up or down by semitone. Useful when a vocalist needs a different key or you're playing with alternate tuning.

Tip

Transpose works on the fly without changing the saved song. Your original key is always preserved.

Streaming Links

Attach links from Spotify, Apple Music, YouTube, or Bandcamp to any song. StageBook auto-detects the service and opens it in the right app with one tap.
