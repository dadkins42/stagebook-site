📄 Songs

Your song library is the heart of StageBook. Everything you need to know about managing your songs.

Adding Songs

Tap the + button to add a new song. You can enter a title, artist, lyrics, and tags. Songs are saved automatically as you type.

📱
Screenshot: Song editor view
💡 Tip

Use section headers like [Verse], [Chorus], and [Bridge] in your lyrics to keep them organized and easy to follow on stage.

Search and Filter

As your library grows, you'll want to find songs quickly. Use the search bar to search by title, artist, or even lyrics content. Use tag filters to narrow down your list.

📱
Screenshot: Search and tag filtering

Sorting

Sort your song list by title, artist, date added, or date modified. Tap the sort button to change the order — it remembers your preference.

Favorites

Mark songs as favorites for quick access. Your favorites appear at the top of the list when you filter by favorites, making it easy to find the songs you perform most often.

Full Screen Mode

When you're on stage, tap the full screen button to see just the lyrics — no distractions, no menus, just the words you need. Swipe or scroll to move through the song.
