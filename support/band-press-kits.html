Building Press Kits

Build a professional electronic press kit to share with venues and promoters.

What Is a Press Kit?

A press kit (or EPK — electronic press kit) is a collection of materials that represent your band to venues, promoters, and media. It typically includes a bio, photos, song samples, and contact information.

📸Screenshot: A band press kit in StageBook

Building Your EPK

StageBook lets you build your EPK right in the app. Add your band bio, upload photos, link to recordings, and include your contact details. Everything is organized into a clean, professional format.

Sharing with Venues

When you're ready to reach out to a venue or promoter, share your press kit directly from StageBook. Recipients get a polished, mobile-friendly view of your band's materials — no attachments or downloads needed.

Tip

Keep your EPK updated — refresh your bio, swap in recent photos, and add new recordings regularly. An outdated press kit can cost you a booking.
