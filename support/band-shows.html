Managing Shows

Book gigs, build setlists, and keep your band prepared for every performance.

Scheduling a Show

Create a show by tapping the + button in the Shows tab. Add the venue, date, time, and any notes your band needs. Everyone in the band will see the show on their calendar.

📸Screenshot: Scheduling a new show

Building a Setlist

Every show can have a setlist — an ordered list of songs your band will perform. Drag songs to reorder, group them into sets, and share the final list with the whole band.

📸Screenshot: Building a setlist for a show

Show Day

On the day of the show, open it from the Shows tab to see your setlist, venue details, and any notes. Swipe through songs during the performance to keep your place.

Tip

Build your setlists early — even a rough draft gives your band time to prepare and suggest changes before the gig.
