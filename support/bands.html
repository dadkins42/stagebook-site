Creating a Band

Tap the Bands tab and tap + to create a new band. Give it a name and you're the band leader. You can invite members by sharing a code or link.

📸Screenshot: Creating a new band

Inviting Members

Share your band's invite code or link with your bandmates. They'll need StageBook installed with a Pro plan to join. Once they join, they'll see all shared content instantly.

📸Screenshot: Invite members screen

Pro plan required

Band features are available with the Pro plan ($4.99/month). All band members need a Pro plan to participate.
