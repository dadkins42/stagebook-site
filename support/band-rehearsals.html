Planning Rehearsals

Schedule rehearsals with a song agenda so your band makes the most of every session.

Scheduling a Rehearsal

Create a rehearsal from the Rehearsals tab. Set the date, time, location, and invite your band. Rehearsals appear on everyone's calendar alongside shows.

📸Screenshot: Scheduling a rehearsal

Song Agenda

Each rehearsal can have a song agenda — a list of songs your band plans to work on. Add songs from your band library, reorder them by priority, and mark songs as covered during practice.

📸Screenshot: Rehearsal song agenda
Tip

Use the song agenda to stay focused during rehearsal. It's easy to jam on favorites — an agenda keeps you working on what matters.
