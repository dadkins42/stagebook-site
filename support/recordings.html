Recordings

Capture ideas, import audio, and keep everything attached to the right song.

Recording a Take

Open any song and tap the microphone icon to start recording. StageBook records directly on your device — no internet needed. Tap stop when you're done, and the recording is automatically attached to that song.

📸Screenshot: Recording interface

Importing Audio Files

Already have recordings from a rehearsal, demo session, or voice memo? Import audio files directly into StageBook and attach them to any song.

📸Screenshot: Import audio flow

Managing Recordings

Each song can have multiple recordings. Swipe to rename or delete recordings. Play them back right from the song detail view.

Tip

Use recordings to capture melody ideas, arrangement notes, or even just a reminder of how you want a section to feel. It's faster than typing.

Recording Quality

StageBook records at high quality using your device's built-in microphone. For best results in noisy environments, consider using headphones with a built-in mic or an external microphone.

Writer plan required

Voice recordings and audio import are available with the Writer plan ($2.99/month) or Pro plan ($4.99/month).
