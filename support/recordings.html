🎤 Recordings

Capture ideas, import audio, and never lose a great take. Recordings live right alongside your songs.

Recording an Idea

Got a melody in your head? A lyric idea? Hit record right from within a song. StageBook captures audio and attaches it directly to the song so it's always easy to find later.

📱
Screenshot: Recording interface

Importing Audio Files

Already have recordings on your phone or computer? Import audio files and attach them to songs. Supports common formats like MP3, M4A, WAV, and more.

📱
Screenshot: Import audio file

Managing Recordings

Each song can have multiple recordings — different versions, different arrangements, different takes. Name them so you can tell them apart, and delete the ones you don't need.

💡 Tip

Name your recordings with context — like "Acoustic version" or "Rehearsal 3/15" — so you remember what each one is when you come back to them weeks later.
